Hello from Alicia Dyas Designs!

I would like to welcome you to my site! I’m a Graphic Designer and Media Artist in Lincoln, NE. Growing up I have always been able to pick up any skill I put my mind to it. All the time I spent focusing on those skills and learning has brought me here to day to be a Graphic Designer. Graphic Design is something I’m passionate about but it’s not the only thing. I’m also very passionate about music, fine art, and literature. I always bring this passion to the table when I’m working on a project. As I’ve explored my passions I’ve discovered a few special skills. These skills are the strengths I bring to my clients as a designer. These strengths include minimal design contrasted with illustration and each are focused on through space. Customized aspects are also a huge part of my strengths.
